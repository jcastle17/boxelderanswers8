Questions to ask

Ask questions that create a record.

General frustration can be dismissed. Specific questions force specific answers.

Use these publicly

Questions for meetings, forms, emails, and public comments.

Submit questions one at a time, save copies, ask for documents, and request plain English answers with source links.

Water

What exact water source will be used?

Name the water right, owner, location, quality, projected annual volume, peak demand, and whether any change application will be filed or refiled.

Cooling

What does closed loop cooling mean in gallons?

Provide initial fill, annual makeup water, heat wave scenario, discharge, wastewater, blowdown, and backup cooling assumptions.

Power

How many gas turbines, engines, and generators are planned?

Break the answer down by phase, fuel source, hours of operation, backup use, testing schedule, and future expansion.

Air

When will emissions modeling be released?

Residents need projected NOx, CO2, particulate, startup, shutdown, emergency, and cumulative emissions before a draft permit becomes the first real disclosure.

Taxes

What public revenue is guaranteed versus projected?

Show incentives, reimbursements, energy tax rates, revenue sharing, infrastructure obligations, and who receives money first.

Control

Who has final authority over future phases?

Identify which decisions stay with Box Elder County, which move through MIDA, which go to state agencies, and where residents can appeal or comment.
